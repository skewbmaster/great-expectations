Philip Pirrip (Pip)

Background
• Both the narrator and the main character of the story
• An orphan being raised by his sister, Mrs. Joe Gargery and her husband, Mr. Joe Gargery, a blacksmith.

Relationships
• Hates his sister who doesn’t treat him well
• Falls in love with Estella, but gets turned down
• Becomes best friends with Herbert, whose father gives accommodation for pip

Plot
• He is working/middle class and after Estella insulted him on his appearance as a child, he hates how he isn’t a gentleman and wishes he had a better family and a better life
• An anonymous man starts to give him money and he becomes a gentleman
• He admits his love to her now he’s a gentleman but gets turned down
• He realises that the man giving him money was a convict he saved as a child
• He comes to a realisation that money and social status aren’t the most important things in life
• He starts to live a humble life with Biddy and Mr. Joe and becomes much happier
• He is greeted by Estella who tells him that she was wrong and her husband who had just died was abusive and horrible to her
• The story ends with them holding hands and Pip says he "saw the shadow of no parting from her."

Mrs. Joe Gargery

Background
• Raised Pip, her brother
• Known for being horrible and arrogant

Relationships
• Hates Pip and constantly reminds him that she put a lot of effort into raising him even though he was a huge hassle to raise
• Possibly made friends with Biddy when they came to help her
• Married to Mr. Joe Gargery, a blacksmith

Plot
• Falls ill and Biddy (an orphan friend of Pip’s) comes to help look after her
• Later she dies of her illness

Estella Mavisham

Background
• Very beautiful but very cold and proud
• An orphan adopted by Miss Havisham
• Upper Class

Relationships
• She ends up marrying Drummle, the most repulsive man Pip knows, but he is abusive and doesn’t treat her well, he later dies
• Has a relatively good relationship with her adopted mother, Miss Havisham
• Childhood friends with Pip

Plot • Makes fun of Pip as a child
• Turns Pip down later in life when he admits his love for her
• Marries Drummle who treats her poorly
• Drummle dies and she tells Pip about what it was like being married to Drummle
• Estella falls in love with Pip (and Pip still loves Estella after all this time)

Miss Havisham

Background
• "a rich and grim lady who lived in a large and dismal house."

Relationships
• Adopted Estella and raised her to play with men’s hearts
• Grows fond of Pip later in the story

Plot
• Towards the start of the play she is horrible to Pip and is very glad when she hears Estella insulting him
• She pretends to be the one giving Pip the money, so she seems like a better person (but Pip eventually finds out who it is giving him the money)
• Eventually changes and gives Pip a large amount of money as ‘compensation’

Magwitch

Background
• A convict – on the run from the police

Relationships
• Pip saves him at a very young age, and he decides to give every cent he earns to Pip to say thank you

Plot
• Pip saves him at the start of the story
• He starts to give Pip money
• He later meets Pip and tells him that he’s been the one giving him money
• Pip hides him from the police, and they try to run off together but when Pip goes to pick him up he sees him getting arrested
• Pip visits him in prison and tells him that his daughter isn’t dead and is beautiful and that he loves her
